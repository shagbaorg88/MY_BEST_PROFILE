About Me

Innovating at the Intersection of Medicine and Technology

I am a board-certified pathologist with over 8 years of clinical experience specializing in hematolymphoid, anatomic, and breast pathology. My unique expertise lies in combining medical diagnostics with advanced data science and software engineering to create transformative healthcare solutions.

As a physician-scientist with dual expertise in medicine and technology, I leverage tools like Python, R, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau to extract meaningful insights from complex medical data, enabling data-driven decision making in clinical practice.

My work focuses on developing innovative approaches to diagnostic pathology, integrating machine learning algorithms with traditional histopathological analysis to improve accuracy, efficiency, and patient outcomes.
